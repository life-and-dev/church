3. Feast of first fruit
   When does the feast of Firstfruit apply?
* “when you come into the land of which I give to you and reap its harvest” (Lev 23:10)
  How?
* You shall eat neither bread nor parched grain nor fresh grain until the same day that you have brought an offering to your God (Lev 23:14)
