2. Feast of the unleavened bread
   "you shall keep it a feast to the LORD throughout your generations; you shall keep it a feast by an ordinance forever" (Exodus 12:14, Exodus 23:15, Exodus 34:18, Numbers 28:17, Ezekiel 45:21)
* Eat unleavened bread 7 days in Abib (15th day of the first month - Lev 23:5) --> remember Israel's exodus out of Egypt: “it was not leavened, because they were driven out of Egypt and could not wait, nor had they prepared provisions for themselves” Exodus 12:39
* 7 days after passover (22nd day of Abib) is Sabbath (do no work)

Psalm: Abib = “green fruit; ears of corn” = Nisan (first month)
