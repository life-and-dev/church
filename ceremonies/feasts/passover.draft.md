# Feast of the Passover (Pesach)
   "In the first [month,] on the fourteenth day of the month, you shall observe the Passover, a feast of seven days; unleavened bread shall be eaten." (Lev 23:5, Numbers 28:16, Ezekiel 45:21, Matthew 26:17-19, 1 Corinthians 5:7-8)
* 14th day is holy convocation: “You shall do no customary work” (Numbers 28:18)
* Eat Passover on evening AFTER 14th day, a.k.a. first Unleavened Bread Feast day (Matthew 26:17)
* Instituted by Yahweh for all generations (Exodus 12:42-43)
* “It [is] a night of solemn observance to the LORD for bringing them out of the land of Egypt” Exodus 12:42
* No foreigner/sojourner/servant shall eat it, unless they are circumcised (Exodus 12:43-45; 48-49)
* Shall be eaten in one house, not outside (Exodus 12:46)
* Shall not break the bones (Exodus 12:47)
* Same laws for applies to strangers (Exodus 12:49)
