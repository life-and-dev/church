# The Evolution of the Church

While the Church has a rich history influenced by various factors, this article primarily focuses on the key events that influenced church traditions, beliefs and rules.

## Christian Influences

| Period      | Influencers                                                                  | Influence                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1st Century | [First Christians](evolution/1st-c-christians.md)                            | The adoption of the Christian name "Christian"                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| 312 AD      | [Emperor Constantine](evolution/312-constantine.md)                          | The Catholic Church was institutionalised<br/>The sun god, Sol Invictus, was considered "Jesus Christ"                                                                                                                                                                               |
| 321         | Constantine decreed "DIES SOLIS"                                             | [Sundays were declared a day of rest.](terms/sundays.md)                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| 325         | [The Nicaea Creed](evolution/325-nicaea-creed.md)                            | Church authority was supported by the government<br/>Christians were obliged to confess Jesus' divinity<br/>The Trinity gets defined<br/>Christian discouraged to keep "Jewish" Passover<br/>Date of Easter formulated                                                               |
| 336         | Pope Julius I                                                                | [The first Christmas was celebrated](evolution/336-christmas.md)                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| 337 AD      | Constantius II and Bishop Ulfilas                                            | Constantius II relaxed the church's creeds which allowed Bishop Ulfilas to [spread Arianism](evolution/337-arianism.md) in the Gothic regions                                                                                                                                        |
| 363         | The Council of Laodicea                                                      | [The Sabbath was outlawed](evolution/363-outlawing-sabbath.md)                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| 380         | The Edict of Thessalonica                                                    | [Christianity became the State Religion of the Roman Empire](evolution/380-state-religion.md)                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| 381         | [The Niceno-Constantinopolitan Creed](evolution/381-nicaea-creed-updated.md) | Declared:<ul><li>the incarnation of Jesus</li><li>all members of the Trinity must be worshipped</li><li>The Holy Spirit is the 3rd member of the Trinity</li><li>The Holy Spirit speaks to the Catholic Church</li><li>There is only 1 church which is the Catholic Church</li></ul> |
| 609         | Pope Boniface IV                                                             | [Feast of "All Holy Martyrs" which led "All Hallows' Eve" (Halloween)](evolution/609-halloween.draft.md)                                                                                                                                                                             |
| 1215        | The Council of Florence                                                      | [The Seven Sacraments](evolution/1215-7-sacraments.md) were defined as necessary for salvation                                                                                                                                                                                       |
