##### Footnote

* All Jewish / Christian based religions require [Repentance](/bible/concepts/repentance) from [sin](/bible/concepts/sin) and [Faith](/life/faith/salvation) in God and His teachings. In addition some either had more laws to live by for [salvation](/bible/concepts/salvation) or claim that God's grace is enough which translate to:
  * Protestants, Oneness Pentecostal, Seventh-day Adventism claim "once saved, always saved" (cannot loose salvation once you had repented)
  * Messianic, laws are not a requirement for salvation, but should at least try to keep them (except temple laws) as an act of gratitude; Deliberate rebellion against these laws could lead to loss of salvation
  * Catholicism, Orthodox, Jehovah's Witnesses, Judaism (Secular), Mormonism claim that salvation is lost if you do not keep certain laws (which depend on the religion)
